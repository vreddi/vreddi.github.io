ECM - RemoteApp

What is Azure Remote App?





Azure RemoteApp brings the functionality of the on-premises Microsoft RemoteApp program, backed by Remote Desktop Services, to Azure. Azure RemoteApp helps you provide secure, remote access to applications from many different user devices.


When you move RemoteApp to Azure, you get to take advantage of the storage, scalability, and global reach of Azure without having to worry about a complex on-premises configuration. Microsoft provides maintenance of Azure, ensuring its reliability, freeing you up to focus on more important issues, like creating the best apps for your business to use. Another advantage of Azure RemoteApp is the accessibility - your users can access RemoteApp programs from Windows, iOS, Mac OS X, and Android devices. They can use your apps in the environment they prefer, while you use the Azure management portal to manage those apps.

MidnightJabber

The main goal for MidnightJabber is to create a social platform that revolves around Movies, TV-Shows, Music and Games. There are a lot of social structures out there which try to do this but they can never dedicate the social platform to just one topic. The social usage always deviates from its initial intension. The only thing closest to a dedicated social structure are subreddits. But they were never mean to be properly structured. The inception mission for MJ was to define that structure.

MidnightJabber has evolved a lot since then. In the process we have built small tools one at a time that tries to tackle the problem. After failing to tackle the problem all at once, taking small steps at a time surely has helped us in gaining insight into the evolution of the online social structure. Currently MJ is a bunch of sub-projects that try to mark its way to its ultimate goal.

"The social structure is evolving. We dont talk on the phone a lot, we text. We dont text a lot, we fb/tweet/whatsapp/groupme... Whats next?"
